About Us

El Shaddai Private School is dedicated to nurturing and educating young minds with a strong emphasis on academic excellence, moral values, and extracurricular activities. Our mission is to create well-rounded individuals prepared for the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for fostering intellectual growth, creativity, and integrity.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and intellectually stimulating environment where students can thrive.

Academics

We offer a diverse curriculum designed to cater to the individual learning styles and needs of our students.

Primary Education

Our primary school curriculum focuses on foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking.

Our Christain Worldview

We nurture our students' foundational skills, character, and curiosity for lifelong learning.

Extracurricular Activities

Our students engage in sports, arts, and community service to develop a balanced character.
